V-SIAH Partner Enablement Program: Empower healthcare advocates, earn incentives, improve outcomes through customer referrals. Join us to make a difference in health management

Why Join the V-SIAH Partner Enablement Program?

Impactful Partnership

Join healthcare enthusiasts, transform health management, empower well-being, promote V-SIAH PHR app, community for healthier lives.

Benefits for Customers

Your referrals will receive an exclusive 10% discount when they sign up for V-SIAH PHR app using your partner link or referral code. Encourage them to experience the benefits of a comprehensive and secure health management solution.

Enhance Your Reputation

By aligning yourself with V-SIAH, a leading PHR app, you solidify your expertise in healthcare technology and showcase your commitment to empowering individuals with Personalized health management solutions

Incentives

Earn a generous 5% incentive for every successful referral made through your unique partner link or referral code. Watch your efforts turn into tangible rewards as you contribute to the growth of V-SIAH

Streamlined Referral Process

Our User-friendly partner dashboard makes it easy for you to track your referrals, monitor your performance, and access promotional materials. We provide you with the tools you need to effectively spread the word about V-SIAH.

How to Get Started

Sign Up

Visit our partner registration page and complete a simple form to join the V-SIAH Partner Enablement Program.

Spread the Word

Utilize your referral links or QR codes codes across various channels such as social media, blogs, or direct communication to share the benefits of V-SIAH PHR app with your network. Leverage our pre-designed marketing materials to make it effortless.

Monitor Your Success

Track the performance of your referrals, view your earned incentives, and gain insights into your impact through your partner dashboard. Celebrate your achievements as you contribute to a healthier future.

Access Your Partner Dashboard

Upon approval, gain access to your personalized partner dashboard, where you can generate your unique referral links or QR codes, track your referrals, and access promotional materials.

Earn Incentives and Provide Benefits

Each time someone signs up for V-SIAH PHR app using your referral link or code, you’ll earn a 5% incentive on the cost. Additionally, your referrals will receive a 10%discount, enhancing their experience right from the start.

Easy Payments:

A Bi-Monthly payment to from the moment of registration directly made to your designated bank accounts. Register to know more about our virtual bank partners like HDFC, ICICI, RBL, and Federal Bank.
